Mission

Tipster is meant as an avenue to view and anonymously report petty crime occurrences that normally go under-reported in the core boroughs of Montreal. It aims to be an instrument to keep the community informed and vigilant. Users can anonymously submit tips through the 'Submit Tip' form, where it will be reviewed by community moderators and posted within 24-48 hours. Tips should be constructive and as detailed as possible without inspiring witch-hunting or doxxing. Examples of tips to submit would be laptop/bike theft, street harrassment, local scams, etc.

Behind Tipster

Tipster is a final project for a web development class and was created by Louis Van Craen, Alexandra Djamen, and Marie Payne.
